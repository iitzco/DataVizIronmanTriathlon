Description

This visualization makes an interactive representation where the arrival of athletes is simulated by competition time, starting at the arrival of the first athlete and ending with the last to arrive. Each athlete is represented as a continuous line where the color represents its category (blue colours correspond to male categories and oranges colours to women) and whose size is proportional to the time of race. It is possible to settle on a line to see the detail of an athlete. It is surprising to see the proportion of athletes who finish the race at each moment. At first they are only professionals with low frequency and a relatively low time while as the simulation progresses, the frequency of arrival of competitors increases as well as the diversity of their categories. The visualization was implemented purely using the D3 library.
